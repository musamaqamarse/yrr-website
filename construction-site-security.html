CONSTRUCTION SITE SECURITY LONDON

Year-Round Security provides a robust solution to construction site safety, ensuring that every aspect of security is meticulously managed. Recognizing the unique challenges of each location, Year-Round Security tailors its services to meet the specific needs of each construction site.

WHAT DOES OUR CONSTRUCTION SITE SECURITY OFFER?


Customized Risk Assessments


Each construction site is unique, and a detailed risk assessment is conducted to consider factors such as location, environment, and specific client requirements to develop a tailored security strategy.

Specialized Security Guarding


Offering a team of highly trained security guards specialized in construction site protection across London, ensuring a secure environment for all stakeholders.

Comprehensive Duty Roster


Security duties encompass perimeter patrols to deter criminal activities, CCTV monitoring, rapid response to incidents, and effective de-escalation of potential threats.

Traffic Management Expertise


Security personnel are proficient in banksman, signaling, and traffic marshalling duties, contributing to the smooth operation of site transport logistics.

Teamwork and Communication


Guards are selected for their ability to collaborate effectively, communicate clearly, and maintain composure under pressure, essential traits for dynamic construction site environments.

Health and Safety Commitment


Recognizing the importance of health and safety on-site, all security guards are SIA approval, receive health and safety training, are fully insured, and undergo rigorous background checks.

Equipment and Training


Equipped with the latest in security technology and gear, including body-worn cameras, PPE, two-way radios, and high-visibility jackets, ensuring guards are prepared for any situation.

Continuous Professional Development


Ongoing training in health and safety best practices is provided, keeping the security team knowledgeable and compliant with current regulations and standards.

DUTIES OUR LONDON CONSTRUCTION SITE SECURITY GUARDS CAN HELP WITH:


Access Control


Manage entry and exit points, ensuring secure access and overseeing traffic flow to prevent congestion and maintain safety.

Rapid response


Act swiftly in response to security alerts or potential threats, ensuring immediate action is taken to safeguard premises and personnel.

Patrol Services


Conduct regular patrols, both internally and externally, to deter unauthorized activity and ensure the security of the property.

CCTV Monitoring


Operate and monitor surveillance equipment, keeping a vigilant eye on different areas of the site to prevent and record any suspicious activity.

Fire and Traffic Marshal Duties


Enforce safety protocols, manage traffic during emergencies, and assist in fire prevention and control measures.

Search Procedures


Perform searches as necessary, following strict procedures to prevent contraband from entering the premises.

Record Keeping


Maintain accurate logs of visitors and goods, ensuring a record of all movements in and out of the site.

First Aid


Provide immediate medical assistance in case of injury, ensuring the well-being of all individuals on site.

Fire Drills and Evacuation


Regularly test fire alarms and conduct evacuation drills to ensure preparedness in case of an emergency.

Incident Reporting


Document any security incidents in detail, providing clear and comprehensive reports for further investigation and action.
