SECURITY GUARDS SERVICE


Year-Round Security takes pride in providing top-notch security services to a wide range of clients. Our security guards are meticulously selected based on their experience, skills, professionalism, and demeanor. Here’s what sets us apart:

  • Licensed and Vetted Guards: All our security officers hold valid SIA licenses and undergo thorough background checks, including criminal records. We prioritize safety and trustworthiness.
  • Tailored Solutions: When assigning guards to your facility, we consider your specific requirements. Whether its plain-clothes officers blending seamlessly or uniformed guards, we adapt to your needs.
  • Multilingual Staff: If your organization communicates in languages other than English, we ensure that our guards speak your language. Effective communication is essential for optimal security.
  • Low Turnover Rate: Our industry-leading low turnover rate ensures continuity. However, we also have highly trained officers on standby for emergencies or absences.
  • Risk Assessments and Security Plans: We conduct detailed risk assessments to identify threats and create customized security plans. Your organization can function optimally within a secure environment.
  • Technology-Enhanced Management: Our use of technology allows close monitoring of our officers. We’re committed to meeting or exceeding your security needs.
