About Us

Year-Round Security stands as a testament to the dedication and expertise of professionals who have honed their skills over a decade in the security industry, with a strong foundation built on front-line, hands-on experience. The commitment to fostering trust and teamwork is at the core of Year-Round Security’s philosophy, ensuring that every partnership is nurtured with the utmost attention to detail and a focus on long-term success.

about Company

Our Primary OBJECTIVES

The primary objective of Year-Round Security is providing a uniquely professional ethical brand of high-quality security services, security administration services, risk and vulnerability assessments to both the private and commercial sectors.

Year-Round Security serves customers by using a flat corporate organizational structure that continuously focuses on and self-evaluates the company’s performance on each individual contract.

Year-Round Security experts are available to evaluate your organization’s security strengths and vulnerabilities as well as your potential security threats and then provide you with a comprehensive security solution… free of charge or commitment. Year-Round Security is committed to and bases its success on the following three key tenets:

  • Recruitment of quality ethical security personnel
  • Tailored training programs varying contract to contract
  • Use of relevant technology
