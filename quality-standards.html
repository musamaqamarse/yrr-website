QUALITY STANDARDS

Attention to detail

  • Identifying, assessing, conceptualizing, designing & implementing security solutions.
  • Threat assessment, risk analysis and recommendations for upgrading of security measures at the facility.
  • Provision of trained, uniformed, and responsive security personnel.
  • Conducting awareness and practical customized training for management staff and employees.
  • Assisting managements to formulate crisis management, emergency response and business continuity plans and manuals.

Delivery As Defined

Today’s growing complexities in the community and the changing needs of clients have raised demand for reliable and tailor-made security solutions. Year-Round Security stands for its consistent delivery of excellent value for money, day after day regardless of who happens to be on the front lines of the delivery process.

Year-Round Security is a quality assured company, and all our security personnel are highly committed to the quality culture within the company and its continuous improvement. Year-Round Security ensures that a positive environment is provided, and all security personnel behave in an appropriate manner and practice appropriate social interactions.

All officers are committed to the Year-Round Security officer competency program which also focuses on individual professional conduct. All officers are evaluated on a regular basis and reports are submitted to the Managing Director.

Year-Round Security believes the system and management model that Year-Round Security has in place for incident management, competencies and training matrix, monitoring and maintaining the quality of operational performance are unique in its way. It is innovative and it helps improve efficiency, provides transparency of security operations and provides our clients with a better understanding of trends within each of the sites. It provides them with an improved understanding of the distribution of incidents over the time of day, day of the week and period of the year.
