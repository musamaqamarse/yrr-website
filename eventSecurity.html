EVENTS SECURITY

Year-Round Security’s dedication to providing comprehensive security services is evident in the breadth of solutions offered. With a focus on versatility and quality, the company ensures that each client’s unique needs are met with precision. The expertise gained from over a decade and a half in the industry translates into meticulously crafted security plans, executed by SIA-certified professionals. Whether the event calls for discreet plain-clothed guards or a more visible uniformed presence, Year-Round Security is equipped to handle the task with the utmost professionalism and efficiency, ensuring peace of mind for all involved.

DUTIES OUR SECURITY EVENT TEAM CAN HELP WITH:


Manned guarding


Plain clothes/uniformed event security team, or a combination of both.

Welcoming and greeting


Smiling, smartly dressed and well-presented security guards are a highly reassuring sight as visitors arrive at your event. Our guards present a warm, comforting welcome to attendees and guests on arrival.

Access control and entry screening


Allowing authorized guests to access the venue and specific designated areas while keeping opportunistic or ill-intentioned undesirable elements out.

Organized parking management


Directing vehicles, preventing congestion, and allowing traffic to flow safely and easily in and out of parking areas.

Crowd management


Helping in crowd direction and control when necessary for the safety of your visitors and to prevent overcrowding.

Body/bag searches


Making sure no unauthorized items enter the event premises.

Ushers


Helping to guide visitors, provide seating assistance.

WHAT DOES OUR EVENT SECURITY SERVICE OFFER?


  • Peace of mind: Primarily your mind can be at ease knowing your event security and the protection of staff and guests are in safe hands.
  • Event duties: If your event requires welcoming and greeting, bag searches, parking management or other duties, we can help. Our teams are more than happy to cater for these duties as well. Just let us know what you need to help you run a seamless event when you contact us.
  • Highly presentable personnel: Our event security guards are smartly dressed, well presented, and well groomed. Our London event security guards have a deep understanding of the subtleties and demands of working with event guests and the public.
  • Trained in customer service: Event security will work with you to ensure that your event runs seamlessly. Our team will provide polite and friendly service and assist you with a variety of event duties, from crowd direction, to organized parking and this support will be tailored to your specific requirements.
